Drag and Drop the pictures from one section to another!

    How to use DRAG and DROP:

  • Hold on to any of the image by clicking on your left mouse button.
  • Drag the picture while keeping the mouse button pressed.
  • Place the picture over the target frame. The target frame is just right to the picture.
  • Release the mouse button to drop the picture.
